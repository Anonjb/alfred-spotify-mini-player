<figure>
	<img src="{{ site.url }}/images/charts2.jpg"></a>
	<figcaption>Choose the country you want.</figcaption>
</figure>

* Then you get the list of tracks from the charts you selected:-

<figure>
	<img src="{{ site.url }}/images/charts3.jpg"></a>
